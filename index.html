Services Provided

Ortho Rehabilitation

Orthopedic rehabilitation provides a range of services to help patients recover from musculoskeletal injuries and surgeries

Neuro Rehabilitation

Neurorehabilitation is a specialized area of healthcare that addresses the needs of individuals with neurological conditions or injuries affecting the nervous system.

Cardio Rehabilitation

Cardiac rehabilitation is a specialized program designed to help individuals recover and improve their heart health after cardiac events or surgeries.

Post Surgical Rehabilitation

Post-surgical rehabilitation, also known as post-operative or surgical rehabilitation, is a crucial aspect of recovery following various types of surgeries.

Joint Pains

Joint pain can be caused by various conditions, including arthritis, injuries, or inflammation.

Shoulder Pains

Shoulder pain can result from various causes, including injuries, overuse, or underlying medical conditions.

Foot Ankle Pain

Foot and ankle pain can stem from various causes, including injuries, structural issues, or medical conditions.Key services typically provided to address foot and ankle pain

Balance Exercise Therapy

Balance exercise therapy is a specialized rehabilitation approach designed to improve an individual's stability and equilibrium. It is often recommended for people with balance issues, such as those recovering from injuries, managing certain medical conditions, or seeking to prevent falls.
